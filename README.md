ğŸ® How to Play
Objective: Match all pairs of Greek symbols before your score reaches zero.

Flipping: Click any card to reveal its symbol. Click a second card to find its match.

Matching: If the symbols match, they stay flipped and turn green.

Penalty: If they don't match, they flip back, and your score decreases.

Winning: Match all 8 pairs to see your final score!
